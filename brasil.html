Brazilian Wild Fire Analysis: 2008-2017

The purpose of this project is to explore Brazilian forest fire data, and to visualize the affected areas. Using data obtained from kaggle,OEC.world, other external resources such as web scraping, and NASA satellite data (NASA FIRMS), we will explore the areas burned from 2008-2017. We are hoping to discover whether or not human activity is the driving force behind all or most of the forest fires. We will also look at different industries that are known for contributing to deforestation and their locations, in an attempt to make correlations between industry and forest fires.
